About Equip

Whole-child support, built around real families.

Equip brings emotional development, counselling and academic support into one joined-up plan for each child. We listen first, understand the child in context, and then build support that fits their real life.

Who We Are

A specialist team with one shared language.

Equip exists for children and families who need support that feels personal, practical and connected. We work with care, curiosity and clear communication, so families know what is happening and why.

Our approach is developmentally minded. That means we look at confidence, learning, emotions, family rhythms and school demands together, then shape the right next step.

Because learning is emotionally driven, and growth requires confidence.

Our team works together so children are not treated as separate pieces of a puzzle. The same child who needs confidence in maths may also need help with anxiety, focus, friendship or big emotions.

Where We Work
