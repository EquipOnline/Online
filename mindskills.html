Mindskills

Emotional intelligence children can actually use.

Mindskills is Equip's structured emotional intelligence programme. It helps children understand themselves, name what they feel and build practical skills for learning, relationships and resilience.

The Programme

A shared language for the whole child.

Mindskills can stand alone as a programme, but it also sits inside the way Equip works. It gives our academic support and counselling teams a shared language when we talk about confidence, regulation, motivation and growth.

Children learn to notice what is happening inside them, connect it to what is happening around them, and practise tools that support better choices.

What Children Build

Self-awareness

Understanding feelings, triggers, strengths, patterns and needs.

Regulation

Practical ways to pause, settle, communicate and recover.

Resilience

Confidence to try again, ask for help and handle setbacks.

How It Fits

Available on its own, or as part of an Equip pathway.

Some children join Mindskills directly. Others meet the programme through academic support or counselling. Either way, the aim is the same: help children become more capable, connected and calm.
