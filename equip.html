How Equip Works

A holistic support programme built around your child.

We start with the child, then build the right mix of support around them.

Some children need academic confidence. Some need emotional language. Some need counselling support. Many need a thoughtful combination of all three.

Equip brings psychology, academic specialists and EQ coaches into one clear programme, so families are not left stitching separate services together on their own.

The Equip Programme

Three support areas, one shared plan.

These are the three parts we draw from when building a programme for your child. Each has its own focus, but they work best when they speak the same language.

Why Families Choose EQUIP

Clear support for the parts of childhood that overlap.

  • Psychology-informed from the start

    We pay attention to confidence, motivation, emotions, school pressure and family context, not just the visible problem.

  • One plan instead of disconnected services

    Your child's academic, emotional and counselling support can work together instead of pulling in different directions.

  • Guidance through the psychosocial side of childhood

    Friendships, motivation, pressure, transitions and self-belief all matter. We help families navigate what does not show up on a report card.

  • Specialist support when needs are layered

    Many children need more than one kind of help. We adapt the pathway to the child rather than forcing the child into a preset service.

  • Support that fits real family life

    Wherever possible, support is shaped around the child's routine, the family's needs and the setting that makes the most sense.

How We Begin

We start by getting to know your child, not their marks.

Before a single lesson happens, we sit down with the family to properly understand who your child is. That's what a developmental approach actually requires.

01

We meet the family

A full conversation about your child's background and history, in their own home context, not a form to fill in.

02

We assess the whole child

Needs, habits, routines, interests and goals. We build a picture of your child, not just their subject gaps.

03

We build the plan

A support plan matched to how your child learns, what motivates them, and what your family needs.

04

We show up

Lessons at home, at school or online. Wherever it works best, delivered with the same holistic approach.
