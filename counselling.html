Counselling

Counselling services for children, adults and families.

Equip counselling supports children, adults, couples and families through anxiety, change, self-esteem, emotional regulation, relationships and difficult experiences.

How It Helps

A calm space to be understood.

Our counselling work is practical, relational and person-centred. We look at what is happening emotionally, socially, at home, in relationships, at school or at work, then shape support around the person's needs.

For children and teens, counselling can connect with Mindskills and academic support where helpful. For adults, couples and families, the focus is on clear support, emotional insight and practical next steps.

Emotions

Support for anxiety, big feelings, confidence, self-esteem, emotional regulation and stress.

Transitions

Guidance through change, school or work stress, friendship strain, relationship pressure, family shifts and new routines.

Relationships

Space for family, relationship and caregiver conversations so support can continue beyond the session.

Free Services

Free online counselling for those in need.

Equip offers free online counselling to people who need support and do not currently have the financial means to access paid counselling services.

To enquire or book free online counselling, please email us or send a WhatsApp message and our team will guide you through the next step.
