Academic Support

Academic support with an EQ lens.

Equip academic support helps children catch up, keep up and grow confidence, while paying attention to how they feel about learning, school and themselves.

What Makes It Different

It is not just tutoring. It is student support.

Every member of our academic team holds an honours degree in psychology. That changes what a session looks like.

We are not only working through a textbook. We are paying attention to confidence, motivation, pressure, learning habits and the emotional blocks that can sit underneath school struggles.

Subject Help

Support for specific school subjects, gaps, study habits and academic goals.

Confidence

Children build a more positive relationship with learning, not only better marks.

Context

We consider the child, family, school rhythm and learning needs together.

Why Families Choose It

Support that understands the whole learner.

  • Psychology-trained specialists

    Subject support is delivered with developmentally aware insight.

  • Family partnership

    Parents are part of the plan from the first conversation onward.

  • Psychosocial school support

    Friendships, motivation, pressure and transitions are included in the picture.

  • Additional learning needs

    Sessions can be adapted for how each child actually works best.
